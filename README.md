# Customer-_Shopping-_Behaviour-_analysis-
An end-to-end data analytics project that analyzes customer shopping behavior using Python, MySQL, and Power BI to deliver actionable business insights
## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes customer shopping behavior based on **3,900 purchase records** to understand shopping patterns, customer preferences, subscription trends.
The workflow covers:
- Data cleaning and preprocessing in **Python**
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Business analysis using **SQL**
- Interactive dashboard development in **Power BI**
- Business recommendations based on data-driven insights

## 🎯 Objectives
 Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Analyze revenue across different customer segments
- Identify top-performing products and categories
- Measure the impact of discounts and subscriptions
- Build an interactive dashboard for business decision-making

- ## 📂 Dataset

- 📄 **Customer Shopping Dataset:** [Shopping_raw_data.csv](./Shopping_raw_data.csv)

The dataset contains **3,900 customer transactions** with **18 features**, including:
- Customer demographics (Age, Gender, Location)
- Purchase details (Product, Category, Amount, Season)
- Shopping behavior
- Subscription status
- Discounts and promotions
- Shipping type
- Customer reviews
- Purchase frequency

# 🔍 Key Insights

- Customer purchasing behavior varies across demographic groups.
- Subscription status influences overall revenue contribution.
- Certain products consistently receive higher customer ratings.
- Discount campaigns affect purchasing patterns differently across categories.
- Loyal and repeat customers contribute significantly to business performance.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis:

- Offer special benefits to encourage more customers to subscribe.
- Reward customers who buy repeatedly to keep them coming back
- Use discounts wisely to increase sales while maintaining good profits.
- Advertise products that have the best ratings and sales..
- Target marketing campaigns toward high-value customer segments.
